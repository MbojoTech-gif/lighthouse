About Us

The Lighthouse Ministers is a vibrant music group affiliated with Jetview SDA Church in Utawala,Nairobi,Kenya.
As part of the Seventh-day Adventist Church we share the message of hope and faith through inspiring and uplifting music.
Join us as we worship, celebrate and spread positivity through music and evangelism,creating an atmosphere of unity and spiritual growth.
